# Party UI Style Guide

- Large mobile-first controls.
- Rounded-looking panels built from bright color blocks and soft shadows.
- Timer compact at the top center.
- Four player score panels use the upper left and upper right safe-area zones.
- Keep the center of the screen mostly clear for readable 3D action.
- Countdown may briefly appear near center, but should stay compact on mobile.
- Result panel should be clear and high contrast without covering the active play area.
- Player colors: blue, red, green, yellow from `PartyArtPalette`.
