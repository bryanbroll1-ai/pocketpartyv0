# Party Art Style Material Presets

The visual style is intentionally bright, low-poly, mobile-friendly, and cartoon-like.

Runtime material presets are defined by:

- `Assets/_Game/Scripts/Visual/PartyArtMaterialType.cs`
- `Assets/_Game/Scripts/Visual/PartyArtPalette.cs`
- `Assets/_Game/Scripts/Visual/PartyArtMaterials.cs`

Preset types:

- Grass
- Dirt
- Wood
- Stone
- Platform
- KitchenCounter
- Water
- Lava
- UIPanel
- PlayerBlue
- PlayerRed
- PlayerGreen
- PlayerYellow
- Skin
- DarkAccent
- WhiteAccent
- Metal
- Sky

These presets use simple Unity materials with soft colors, light smoothness, and small emission highlights only for important objects such as water, lava, and player colors.
